Welcome to Jaskirat's Roulette

How to play

  • Click a chip to choose a bet amount ($1, $5, $25, $100 or custom)
  • Tap any number (0–36) or outside bets (RED/BLACK, EVEN/ODD, 1st 12 / 2nd 12 / 3rd 12) to place a bet.
  • You can stack chips on the same spot — badges show the total bet there.
  • When ready, press Spin. Wheel spins clockwise for ~15s; ball spins anticlockwise and lands in a pocket.
  • Payouts: Straight number 35:1 (returns 36× stake), Dozens 2:1 (3×), Even/Odd & Color 1:1 (2×).
